Hi, I'm Viet Le Duy — a Creative Director and founder of Mighty Stone.

I work with brands and teams to craft meaningful visuals that blend creativity, design, and storytelling. I believe every brand has its own character and untapped possibilities. My goal is to uncover that essence and transform it into work that stands out — and creates real value.

"Creativity is not about adding more — it's about uncovering what's already there, waiting to be seen."

The Journey

I’ve been drawn to creating things from a very young age — not just for the joy of making, but for the idea that what you create can serve a real purpose. Early on, I became fascinated by how visuals, systems, and ideas could come together to solve problems and communicate meaning.

Over time, that curiosity grew into a clear direction: using creativity as a tool for businesses. Not decoration, not trends — but thoughtful visual solutions that help brands explain what they do, connect with people, and move forward with confidence.

That belief eventually became Mighty Stone — a creative studio built to deliver meaningful services, not just outputs. We create products, visuals, and stories that are designed to work: to clarify messages, strengthen brands, and support real business goals.

Alongside serving clients, I’m also building something just as important to me — a creative environment where people can grow, think deeply, and do work they’re proud of. A place where creativity is respected, problems are worth solving, and good work is the standard.

This journey is ongoing. And everything I build today is shaped by that same idea: creation should always matter.

Mighty Stone

Mighty Stone isn't just a studio — it's a philosophy. Founded on the belief that every brand has an untold story worth discovering, we work with companies who aren't afraid to stand out and make a real impact.

We're a team of creative thinkers, strategic minds, and visual storytellers who believe that great work comes from deep collaboration. We don't just execute briefs — we partner with our clients to uncover what makes them unique and translate that into work that resonates.

From startups finding their voice to established brands reinventing themselves, we bring clarity, creativity, and strategic thinking to every project. Our approach is simple: listen deeply, think boldly, create purposefully.
